========================================
NGO INTRODUCTION
========================================

NGO Name:
InAmigos Foundation


About NGO:
InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded in 2020 by Govind Shukla. The organization focuses on creating sustainable social impact through community-driven initiatives across India. The foundation works in areas such as education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and humanitarian support.


Mission Statement:
The mission of InAmigos Foundation is to empower communities and create positive social change through awareness, support programs, volunteer activities, and sustainable development initiatives.


Vision Statement:
To build an inclusive and compassionate society where every individual has access to opportunities, support, education, and a better quality of life.

PROJECTS SECTION

PROJECT 1

Project Name:
Project Udaan

Description:
Project Udaan focuses on women empowerment through education, skill development, awareness programs, and financial independence initiatives. The project aims to help women build confidence, gain opportunities, and lead empowered lives.

Impact:
Promoting gender equality and empowering women through sustainable support systems.

PROJECT 2

Project Name:
Project Jeev

Description:
Project Jeev is dedicated to animal welfare and protection. The initiative focuses on feeding stray animals, spreading awareness about animal care, and encouraging compassion towards abandoned and injured animals.

Impact:
Creating a safer and more compassionate environment for animals in society.

PROJECT 3

Project Name:
